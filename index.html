Why Netrebko

Why Anna Netrebko is one of the best opera singers of modern days?

Throughout her career, Anna Netrebko has become the prima donna of the 21st century for her powerful and yet silky voice and for her dramatic performances on stage.
In live performance and on numerous award-winning recordings, Netrebko’s portrayals of opera’s most iconic heroines demonstrate a remarkable range and artistic sensibility. Her interpretations span from classical Mozart operas to bel canto masterpieces like Puccini, Verdi, Tchaikovksy and Wagner's operas, among others.
